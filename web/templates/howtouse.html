How to Use the Object Detection App

Follow these detailed steps to fully utilize the features of the Object Detection App and experience real-time object analysis:

Step 1: Upload an Image

Navigate to the homepage and click on the "Upload" button. Select an image from your device to analyze. The app will instantly process the image and provide detailed results, including identified objects and their classifications.

Step 2: Upload a Video

Choose a video file from your device to perform frame-by-frame object detection. This feature is perfect for analyzing videos and extracting detailed insights.

Step 3: Use Live Detection

Activate the live detection feature by granting the app access to your device's camera. This allows real-time object analysis, perfect for dynamic environments or on-the-go scenarios.

Step 4: View Results

Once the detection process is complete, you can view detailed results such as object names, confidence scores, and bounding boxes. This information is presented in an easy-to-read format.

Step 5: Provide Feedback

We value your input! Use the "Feedback" section to share your thoughts, suggestions, or report any issues. Your feedback helps us enhance the app's functionality and performance.
